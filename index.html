My Tax Administrator

Taxes are indispensable for the functioning of a government, providing the necessary revenue to fund public services, infrastructure, and social programs. Acknowledging our importance in the process is crucial for sustaining a well-functioning society. We developed this MiniPRN administrator, a mobile app that helps streamline the tax collection process by reducing redundancies through incorporating QR Codes on the e-payment slips and integrated the app with the Lipa Na Mpesa API to process all mpesa payments. This saves taxpayers' time and effort during the process and customs clearings can be done much faster without any payment hiccups whatsoever.
